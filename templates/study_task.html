In order to get started with UKP citizen science projects, you first need to sign up through Clarin. Clarin is a service that allows anyone to be able to participate in a variety of scientific efforts. You can think of it as a log-in service to a number of language-based tools, including our platform INCEpTION. Note that we will not save nor make use of any personal data you provide to Clarin.
